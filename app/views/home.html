Welcome to Hatchling

Hatchling is an attempt to recreate the television experience through the internet. Create your own channel, or surf through other user created channels to find videos that you enjoy.

Create Dynamic Playlists

Share your favourite videos with the world. You can create and moderate your own mini tv channel, whether you want to share a playlist of your favourite gaming, news, sports, music or other type of video it is up to you.
